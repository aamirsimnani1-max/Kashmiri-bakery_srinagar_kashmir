## About
Kashmiri Bakery & Sweets is a family-owned bakery located in Srinagar, Kashmir, dedicated to baking fresh breads, traditional Kashmiri sweets, and fusion treats using local ingredients and time-honored recipes. We focus on quality, freshness, and authentic flavors.

## Specialties
- Sheermal (traditional saffron flatbread)
- Girda & Lavasa (soft traditional breads)
- Shufta (Kashmiri dry fruit sweet)
- Phirni (creamy rice pudding)
- Kulcha and stuffed breads
- Fresh cakes, cookies, and pastries (daily specials)

## Menu Highlights
- Breakfast: Freshly baked breads, kulcha, stuffed parathas
- Sweets: Shufta, Phirni, Gulab Jamun, and seasonal traditional sweets
- Bakery: Sourdough / country loaves, Sheermal, cookies, muffins
- Beverages: Kashmiri Noon Chai (salty pink tea), Kahwa (Kashmiri green tea)
